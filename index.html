About Us

Who We Are

Mono Education Ltd is an educational consultancy and development partner committed to advancing education and sustainable development. We collaborate with governments, educational organizations, private sector and technology companies, foundations, NGOs, universities, research institutions, development partners, and international organizations.

Through innovative programs and initiatives, we leverage education to address social, economic, and humanitarian challenges, fostering long-term impact in local and global communities.
